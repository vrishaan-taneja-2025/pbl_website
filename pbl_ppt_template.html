CropCare

"An AI-powered web-based system for early crop disease detection using deep learning and structured agricultural knowledge."

Problem Statement

Crop diseases significantly impact agricultural productivity, particularly in rural regions where expert diagnosis is unavailable. Manual disease identification is slow, error-prone, and often results in delayed treatment and crop loss.

Literature Review

Existing solutions rely on expert inspection or computationally heavy CNN models such as VGG and ResNet, limiting real-time usability.

Research Gap

CropCare addresses this gap by using MobileNetV2 for efficient disease classification combined with a centralized information system.

System Methodology

Dataset

PlantVillage dataset containing labeled images of healthy and diseased crop leaves, preprocessed and augmented.

Model

MobileNetV1-based CNN trained for multi-class crop disease classification with confidence scoring.
